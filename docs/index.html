AI Workflow & Vibe Researching

Yiyang Jiang published on 2026-07-06 included in Journal Club
PresenterYiyang Jiang
DateJuly 6, 2026
TopicAI Workflow & Vibe Researching

The presentation argues that for modern physics research, the key question is no longer whether to use AI, but how to use it effectively. It distinguishes two major roles: NN-based AI for principles (neural-network fitting, neural quantum states, DeepH) and LLM-based AI for workflow (planning, memory, tools, APIs, MCP, agent loops).
